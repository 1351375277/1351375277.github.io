电源对比，从左到右依次是：iPad 用的 10 瓦电源、MacBook 的 29 瓦 USB-C 电源、MacBook Air 用的 60 瓦 Magsafe 2 电源。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5855" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][2]

USB-C 接口特写

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5856" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][3]

和上一个叫 MacBook 的苹果笔记本（ 2011 年停产）对比。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5857" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][4]

还是从底壳拆起，苹果用的是对拆机不友好的五角螺丝。但是想想看，一个内存、硬盘都焊死且没有风扇不用清灰的笔记本，普通用户干嘛拆它？

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5858" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][5]

打开外壳，注意别一下子掀开，因为连着数据线呢。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5860" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][6]

断开键盘、触控板的数据线

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5861" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][7]

现在可以完全打开外壳了，但靠近转轴一侧还有很多条线缆需要断开。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5862" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][8]

电池线缆接口藏在主板下面，以前苹果在 iPad 上用过这样的设计。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5863" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][9]

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5864" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][10]

断开主板和电源的连接

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5865" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][11]

这块三角螺丝固定的金属板下面，是（唯一的那个） USB-C 和主板的数据线接口

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5866" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][12]

取下螺丝

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5867" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][13]

断开 USB-C 接口和主板的连接

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5868" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][14]

断开视频数据线接口

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5869" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][15]

断开音频线接口

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5870" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][16]

后盖终于彻底分离了

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5871" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][17]

取下 MacBook 的主板，没错，就这么一小条。比 MacBook Air 的主板还要小 67%

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5872" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][18]

拆下 CPU 外的散热器，Intel 的新处理器发热之低不需要散热风扇。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5873" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][19]

散热器露出 CPU，散热器多点与主板相连，以便把热量导出去。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5874" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][20]

MacBook 主板正面上的芯片：

  * 红框 &#8211; 尔必达/镁光 8GB 移动内存
  * 橙框 &#8211; 东芝 128GB 闪存（背面还有 128GB）
  * 黄框 &#8211; 恩智浦微控制器；128kb 闪存，10kB SRAM
  * 绿框 &#8211; 温度传感器
  * 蓝框 &#8211; 德州仪器 SN6508，可能是功率转化器

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5875" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][21]

MacBook 主板背面上的芯片：

  * 英特尔 Core M-5Y31 处理器（双核，1.1GHz，可睿频至 2.4GHz），内置英特尔 HD Graphics 5300 集成显卡
  * 橙框 &#8211; 海力士 4Gb（512MB）LPDDR3-SDRAM
  * 黄框 &#8211; 东芝 128 GB 闪存
  * 绿框 &#8211; 尔必达/镁光 J4216EFBG-GNL-F DDR3 SDRAM
  * 浅蓝框 &#8211; 博通 BCM15700A2 （可能是）无线网络芯片
  * 蓝框 &#8211; Murata 339S0250 （可能是） Wi-Fi 模块
  * 紫框 &#8211; 980 YFE TM4EA231 H6ZXRI 49AQN5W GI

&nbsp;

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5876" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][22]

MacBook 的电池是分片粘在机身上的，这种阶梯分部技术能更充分利用机身空间，多提供 35% 的电量（一整块的话，很多边边角角的空间就塞不进去浪费了，尤其别忘了 MacBook 是弧线形机身），拆机时要用力撬下来。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5881" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][23]

MacBook 的所有电池，7.55V、39.71Wh、5263 mAh（更厚的 MacBook Air 11&#8243; 的电池是 5100 mAh）。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5882" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][24]

苹果 MacBook 的音箱（不会是金的吧），在靠近转轴的位置。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5883" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][25]

粗线是音箱的电力/信号排线，细的是 MacBook 的天线。iFixit 怀疑苹果其实把音箱的金属壳也当做了天线的一部分。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5884" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][26]

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5885" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][27]

拆下苹果的最新科技：Force Touch 触控板，能感知压力大小做出不同操作、能给手指以震动回馈。更神奇的是，明明为了节省空间不能按下这块触控板，但通过震动反馈，你点击时根本意识不到在按一块不能动的铝板 …… 明明感觉到按下去了。

题外话，我前几天在同事的电脑上亲自体验过，果然很赞。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5886" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][28]

背面的这东西就是 Force Touch 的反馈引擎

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5887" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][29]

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5888" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][30]

几张特写

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5889" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][31]

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5890" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][32]

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5891" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][33]

触控板下面的芯片：

  * 红框 &#8211; 博通 BCM5976 触控屏控制器
  * 橙框 &#8211; ST Microelectronics 32F103 ARM Cortex-M 微控制器
  * 黄框 &#8211; Linear Technology LT3954 LED 转换器

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5892" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][34]

拆下 USB-C 的数据线

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5893" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][35]

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5894" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][36]

拆下耳机孔对应的接口模块和数据线

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5895" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][37]

键盘的固定螺丝非常小，和 iPhone 上用的差不多。

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5898" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][38]

拆下螺丝后，撕开键盘底板，又是胶水 ……

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5900" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][39]

撬下键帽看里面的结构，这块苹果全新设计的键盘，键帽更大，键程极短。而且每个键下方都有个独立的 LED 背光灯，而不是以前那样全键盘共用一排灯。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5901" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][40]

新设计的蝴蝶型键帽支架。题外话，据用过的人说，手感像在 iPad 触控屏上打字。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5902" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][41]

开始拆下屏幕

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5903" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][42]

USB-C 接口模块，除了音频口和 WiFi、蓝牙，剩下的充电、数据、图像等等对外交换全都靠它了。

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5904" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][43]

USB-C 接口模块特写

[<img class="attachment-full" src="http://cache.maoshu.cc//wp-content/uploads/2015/04/attachment.phpfid5906" alt="attachment" width="592" height="444" />][44]

键盘下的芯片：

  * 红框 &#8211; 4 颗德州仪器的  TLC5951 LED 驱动器
  * 橙框 &#8211; 一对 NXP PCAL6416A I/O Expander
